ABOUT LUMIÈRE
================================================================

What is Lumière?
Lumière is a premium skincare brand founded in 2018, dedicated to crafting
science-backed, clean beauty products that deliver visible results. Every
formula is developed with leading dermatologists and tested on all skin types.

What makes Lumière different from other skincare brands?
We combine pharmaceutical-grade active ingredients with clean, plant-based
carriers. Our products are free from parabens, sulphates, synthetic fragrances,
mineral oils, and microplastics. Every product undergoes 18 months of clinical
testing before launch.

Are Lumière products cruelty-free?
Yes. Lumière is certified cruelty-free by Leaping Bunny. We never test on
animals and do not sell in markets that require animal testing.

Are Lumière products vegan?
95% of our range is vegan. The exceptions are our Renewal Eye Cream and Royal
Honey Mask, which contain ethically sourced beeswax and honey. These products
are clearly labelled on our website.

Are Lumière products suitable for sensitive skin?
Yes. All products are dermatologist-tested and formulated for sensitive skin
compatibility. We recommend patch testing any new product on your inner arm
for 24-48 hours before full use. If you have a known allergy, review the full
ingredient list on our website before purchasing.

Are Lumière products safe during pregnancy?
We recommend consulting your doctor or dermatologist before using any skincare
products during pregnancy. Our Vitamin C Glow Serum and Retinol Night Cream
contain ingredients that are typically avoided during pregnancy. Our Hydra-Calm
Cleanser, Moisture Surge Moisturiser, and SPF 50 Glow Shield are generally
considered safe, but please always seek medical advice.

Where are Lumière products made?
All Lumière products are formulated and manufactured in our GMP-certified
facility in Lausanne, Switzerland. Our packaging is produced in Portugal.

PRODUCTS & INGREDIENTS
================================================================

What are your hero products?
- Lumière Vitamin C Glow Serum — brightening, antioxidant protection, 15% L-ascorbic acid
- Lumière Retinol Night Cream — anti-ageing, cell renewal, 0.5% encapsulated retinol
- Lumière Hydra-Calm Cleanser — gentle cleansing for sensitive and reactive skin
- Lumière Moisture Surge Moisturiser — 72-hour hydration with hyaluronic acid complex
- Lumière SPF 50 Glow Shield — daily protection with a luminous, non-greasy finish
- Lumière Renewal Eye Cream — reduces dark circles and puffiness, peptide-enriched
- Lumière Royal Honey Mask — weekly deep nourishment and repair treatment
- Lumière AHA Resurfacing Toner — exfoliates and refines texture with 10% glycolic acid

What ingredients do you use?
Key actives across our range include:
- Vitamin C (L-ascorbic acid) — brightening and antioxidant
- Retinol (encapsulated) — anti-ageing and cell renewal
- Hyaluronic acid (3 molecular weights) — deep and surface hydration
- Niacinamide — pore-minimising, oil control, barrier support
- Peptides (Matrixyl 3000, Argireline) — firmness and fine line reduction
- AHAs (glycolic and lactic acid) — exfoliation and texture refinement
- Ceramides — barrier repair and protection
- Bakuchiol — natural retinol alternative for sensitive skin
- Centella Asiatica — soothing and redness reduction
- Squalane — lightweight moisturisation and barrier support

Can I use the Vitamin C Serum and Retinol Night Cream together?
Yes, but use them at different times of day. Apply the Vitamin C Glow Serum in
the morning under your moisturiser and SPF. Apply the Retinol Night Cream in
the evening. Do not layer them together as they can destabilise each other.

Can I use the AHA Toner every day?
We recommend starting 2-3 times per week and building up to daily use over
4-6 weeks as your skin adapts. Always use SPF during the day when using AHAs
as they increase sun sensitivity. Do not use on the same evening as retinol.

What order should I apply my Lumière products?
Morning: Cleanser → AHA Toner (if using) → Vitamin C Serum → Eye Cream →
Moisturiser → SPF 50 Glow Shield.
Evening: Cleanser → AHA Toner (if using, alternate with retinol nights) →
Retinol Night Cream → Eye Cream → Moisturiser.
Weekly: Royal Honey Mask after cleansing, before serum.

Do your products contain fragrance?
No synthetic fragrances. Some products contain naturally-derived botanical
scents from ingredients like rosehip and bakuchiol. Full ingredient lists are
available on each product page.

How long do Lumière products last once opened?
Most products have a 12-month period after opening (PAO symbol on packaging).
The Vitamin C Serum has a 6-month PAO due to the instability of L-ascorbic acid.
Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight for best results.

My Vitamin C Serum has turned orange. Is it still good?
Oxidation causes the serum to darken from pale yellow to orange or brown.
An oxidised serum loses its efficacy and should be replaced. To prevent this,
store in a dark, cool place and ensure the lid is tightly closed after each use.

SKIN REACTIONS & CONCERNS
================================================================

I had a reaction to a Lumière product. What should I do?
Stop using the product immediately. Rinse the affected area with cool water.
If you experience severe swelling, difficulty breathing, or a severe rash,
seek medical attention immediately. For mild reactions, contact us at
support@lumiereskin.com — we will issue a full refund and our team of
estheticians can advise on alternatives suitable for your skin type.

Is it normal for my skin to purge when I start using the Retinol Night Cream?
Skin purging can occur with retinol, especially in the first 2-6 weeks of use.
This involves increased breakouts or flaking as your skin accelerates cell
turnover. Purging is temporary. If it persists beyond 8 weeks or is severe,
discontinue use and contact us. To minimise purging, start with every other
night application and build up gradually.

My skin is peeling after using the AHA Toner. Is this normal?
Mild flaking is normal when introducing AHAs. Reduce frequency to 1-2 times
per week until your skin adjusts. Always moisturise after using the toner and
wear SPF during the day. If peeling is severe or accompanied by redness and
burning, discontinue use and contact us.

Can I use Lumière products if I have eczema or rosacea?
Many customers with eczema and rosacea use our Hydra-Calm Cleanser and
Moisture Surge Moisturiser successfully. We recommend avoiding AHAs, retinol,
and vitamin C formulas until your skin condition is stable. We always recommend
consulting your dermatologist before introducing new products.
